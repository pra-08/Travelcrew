Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh to Varanasi

Embark on your spiritual journey from Delhi/Chandigarh towards the holy city of Varanasi. Board the Volvo from the designated pick-up point and enjoy a comfortable overnight journey.

Day 2:Varanasi Arrival & Sightseeing

Arrive in Varanasi, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, known for its spiritual significance and ancient temples. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Later, visit the sacred ghats of the Ganges River, including Dashashwamedh Ghat and Manikarnika Ghat, to witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony. Explore the narrow alleys and bustling markets of the Old City. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Varanasi.

Day 3:Varanasi to Ayodhya

After breakfast, proceed to Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Rama and a revered pilgrimage destination for Hindus. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel and relax for a while. Then, visit the iconic Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, where Lord Rama was believed to have been born, and the Hanuman Garhi Temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. Explore other significant sites such as Kanak Bhawan and Nageshwarnath Temple. In the evening, immerse yourself in the serene atmosphere of the Saryu River ghats. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Ayodhya.

Day 4:Ayodhya Sightseeing

Begin your day with a visit to the beautiful banks of the Saryu River for a peaceful morning ritual or boat ride. Explore more of Ayodhya's rich cultural and religious heritage by visiting the Kanak Bhawan, Hanuman Garhi, and Nageshwarnath Temple. Dive into the historical significance of the city with a visit to the Ayodhya Archaeological Museum. Spend the evening exploring the local markets and sampling traditional Ayodhya cuisine. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5:Departure

After breakfast, bid farewell to the sacred city of Ayodhya as you head back to Delhi/Chandigarh via Volvo. Depart with divine blessings and cherished memories of your spiritual journey through Varanasi and Ayodhya.
