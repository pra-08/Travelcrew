Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Port Blair

Your adventure to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands begins with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Port Blair. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Check-in, freshen up, and rest for a while. In the evening, visit the Cellular Jail, a historic landmark that bears witness to India's struggle for independence. Attend the captivating Light and Sound Show that narrates the tale of the island's tumultuous past. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 2:Port Blair - Ross Island - North Bay Island

After breakfast, embark on a full-day excursion to Ross Island and North Bay Island. Start with a visit to Ross Island, once the administrative headquarters of the British in the Andaman Islands. Explore the ruins of the British buildings and enjoy the scenic beauty of the island. Next, head to North Bay Island, known for its coral reefs and underwater marine life. Enjoy snorkeling and glass-bottom boat rides to witness the vibrant coral gardens and colorful fish. Return to Port Blair in the evening. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 3:Port Blair - Havelock Island

After breakfast, proceed to the Port Blair Harbor to board a ferry to Havelock Island, known for its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. Later, visit the world-famous Radhanagar Beach, ranked as one of Asia's best beaches. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and beachcombing on the powdery white sands. In the evening, witness a mesmerizing sunset at the beach. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

Day 4:Havelock Island - Elephant Beach

After breakfast, embark on an excursion to Elephant Beach, known for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. You can reach the beach by a short boat ride or opt for a thrilling trek through the dense forest. Enjoy snorkeling and swimming in the pristine waters, exploring the underwater world filled with colorful corals and tropical fish. After a day of adventure, return to Havelock Island and relax at your hotel. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

Day 5:Havelock Island - Neil Island

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed to the Havelock Island Harbor to board a ferry to Neil Island. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and unwind. Later, visit the stunning beaches of Neil Island, including Bharatpur Beach, Sitapur Beach, and Laxmanpur Beach. Enjoy leisurely walks along the sandy shores, watching the waves crash against the rocks. In the evening, witness a breathtaking sunset at Laxmanpur Beach. Overnight stay in Neil Island.

Day 6:Neil Island - Port Blair

After breakfast, explore more of Neil Island's attractions, including the natural rock formations at Natural Bridge and the serene beauty of Howrah Bridge. Later, return to Port Blair by ferry. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. Spend the evening exploring the local markets and shopping for souvenirs. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 7:Port Blair - Baratang Island - Port Blair

Today, embark on a full-day excursion to Baratang Island, known for its limestone caves and mangrove forests. Start early in the morning and drive through the dense forests of the Andaman Islands to reach Baratang. Visit the mesmerizing limestone caves at Limestone Cave and explore the mangrove creeks by boat at Parrot Island. Return to Port Blair in the evening. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 8:Departure from Port Blair to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands as you transfer to the Port Blair Airport for your return flight to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with cherished memories of your island adventure, promising to return for more exploration in the future.
