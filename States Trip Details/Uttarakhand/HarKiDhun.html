Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Sankri

Your adventure to Har ki Dun begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Sankri, the base camp for the Har ki Dun trek. Prepare for an exciting journey into the heart of the Himalayas, filled with breathtaking landscapes and serene wilderness.

Day 2:Arrival in Sankri and Trek to Taluka

Arrive in Sankri in the morning and take a short drive to Taluka, the starting point of the Har ki Dun trek. Enjoy breakfast amidst the picturesque surroundings of Taluka and prepare for the trek ahead. Begin your trek through dense forests of pine and oak trees, following the gushing Supin River. Traverse through charming villages and terraced fields, soaking in the rustic beauty of the Himalayan landscape. Reach Taluka by afternoon and set up camp for the night. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Taluka.

Day 3:Trek from Taluka to Osla

After breakfast, resume your trek towards Osla, a scenic village nestled along the banks of the Supin River. Trek through lush meadows adorned with colorful wildflowers, with towering peaks looming in the distance. En route, visit the picturesque village of Gangad, known for its traditional wooden architecture and warm hospitality. Continue your journey to Osla and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this remote Himalayan village. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Osla.

Day 4:Trek from Osla to Har ki Dun

Today marks the highlight of your trek as you journey to Har ki Dun, a beautiful valley surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Begin your ascent towards Har ki Dun, following the trail alongside the Supin River. Marvel at the panoramic vistas of the surrounding mountains and keep an eye out for wildlife such as Himalayan langurs and musk deer. As you approach Har ki Dun, the landscape transforms into a lush alpine meadow dotted with wildflowers. Reach Har ki Dun by afternoon and revel in the breathtaking beauty of this Himalayan paradise. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Har ki Dun.

Day 5:Exploration and Rest Day at Har ki Dun

Today is reserved for exploration and relaxation in Har ki Dun. Wake up to majestic views of the surrounding peaks and spend the day exploring the enchanting meadows and glacial streams of Har ki Dun. You can take leisurely walks to nearby viewpoints, interact with local shepherds, or simply soak in the serene ambiance of this pristine Himalayan valley. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Har ki Dun.

Day 6:Descent from Har ki Dun to Osla

After breakfast, bid farewell to the picturesque landscapes of Har ki Dun as you begin your descent back to Osla. Retrace your steps through the scenic trail, relishing the memories of your trek to this remote Himalayan valley. Upon reaching Osla, spend the evening relaxing and reminiscing about your adventure-filled journey. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Osla.

Day 7:Trek from Osla to Taluka and Departure to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, embark on the final leg of your trek as you descend from Osla to Taluka. Enjoy the scenic views and tranquil surroundings one last time as you make your way back to Taluka. Upon reaching Taluka, bid farewell to the majestic Himalayas and board your transport for the journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with a heart full of memories and a sense of accomplishment from your unforgettable trek to Har ki Dun.
