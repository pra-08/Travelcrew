Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Haridwar

Your sacred journey to the Chardham begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Haridwar, the gateway to the Char Dham Yatra. Prepare for a spiritual odyssey filled with divine blessings and enchanting landscapes.

Day 2:Arrival in Haridwar and Visit to Har Ki Pauri

Arrive in Haridwar in the morning and check into your accommodation. After refreshing, visit Har Ki Pauri, one of the holiest ghats on the banks of the Ganges River. Participate in the Ganga Aarti ceremony, where priests offer prayers to the river with lighted lamps, creating a mesmerizing spectacle. Immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance of Haridwar and seek blessings for your Chardham journey. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day 3:Haridwar to Barkot

After breakfast, depart from Haridwar and proceed towards Barkot, the base camp for the Yamunotri pilgrimage. En route, visit the Prakateshwar Cave, a sacred cave dedicated to Lord Shiva. Continue your journey through scenic landscapes, crossing picturesque towns and villages. Upon reaching Barkot, check into your accommodation and rest for the night. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Barkot.

Day 4:Barkot to Yamunotri and Return to Barkot

Today, embark on a pilgrimage to Yamunotri, the source of the Yamuna River and one of the Char Dhams. Begin with a trek from Janki Chatti to Yamunotri, surrounded by majestic mountains and dense forests. Pay homage at the Yamunotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Yamuna, and take a holy dip in the thermal springs of the Surya Kund. After offering prayers, trek back to Janki Chatti and return to Barkot. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Barkot.

Day 5:Barkot to Uttarkashi

After breakfast, proceed from Barkot to Uttarkashi, a picturesque town nestled on the banks of the Bhagirathi River. Visit the renowned Vishwanath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and seek blessings for your Chardham journey. Explore the local markets of Uttarkashi and indulge in shopping for souvenirs. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Uttarkashi.

Day 6:Uttarkashi to Gangotri and Return to Uttarkashi

Today, embark on a pilgrimage to Gangotri, the origin of the sacred Ganges River. Drive through breathtaking landscapes and reach Gangotri, where you'll visit the Gangotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Ganga. Offer prayers at the temple and take a holy dip in the icy waters of the Bhagirathi River. Afterward, trek back to Uttarkashi and spend the evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Uttarkashi.

Day 7:Uttarkashi to Guptkashi

After breakfast, depart from Uttarkashi and proceed towards Guptkashi, a scenic town located on the way to Kedarnath. En route, marvel at the beauty of the Himalayan ranges and the lush greenery of Uttarakhand. Upon reaching Guptkashi, check into your accommodation and rest for the night. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 8:Guptkashi to Kedarnath

Today marks the pilgrimage to Kedarnath, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a significant destination of the Char Dham Yatra. Begin your journey with a drive to Gaurikund, followed by a trek or a helicopter ride to Kedarnath. Visit the ancient Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and seek blessings at this sacred shrine. Spend the day exploring the mystical surroundings of Kedarnath and immerse yourself in the divine ambiance. Dinner and overnight stay at a guesthouse in Kedarnath.

Day 9:Kedarnath to Guptkashi

After the morning rituals and darshan at the Kedarnath Temple, begin your descent back to Guptkashi. Reflect on the spiritual experiences gained during your pilgrimage to Kedarnath as you journey through the scenic landscapes of Uttarakhand. Upon reaching Guptkashi, check into your accommodation and relax for the night. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 10:Guptkashi to Badrinath

After breakfast, proceed from Guptkashi to Badrinath, the final destination of the Chardham Yatra. En route, stop at the sacred Vishnu Prayag, one of the Panch Prayags where the Alaknanda River meets the Dhauliganga River. Continue your journey and reach Badrinath by evening. Check into your accommodation and prepare for the darshan at the Badrinath Temple the next day. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Badrinath.

Day 11:Badrinath Sightseeing

Today, visit the iconic Badrinath Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, and offer prayers at this revered shrine. Explore other significant attractions in Badrinath, including the Mana Village, the last village before the Indo-Tibetan border, and the Vyas Cave, where Sage Vyas is believed to have composed the Mahabharata. Spend the day soaking in the spiritual vibes of Badrinath and the majestic beauty of the surrounding Himalayas. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Badrinath.

Day 12:Departure from Badrinath to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the divine town of Badrinath as you commence your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the sacred experiences and divine blessings received during your Chardham Yatra. Depart with a heart full of gratitude and reverence for the spiritual journey undertaken in the divine abode of Uttarakhand.
