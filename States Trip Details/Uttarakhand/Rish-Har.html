Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Haridwar

Begin your spiritual journey with a departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Haridwar. Board your designated transport and embark on a scenic drive to Haridwar, a sacred city on the banks of the Ganges River. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and take some time to relax. In the evening, witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, a spiritual experience filled with music, lights, and prayers. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day 2:Haridwar Exploration

After breakfast, immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance of Haridwar. Visit the ancient Mansa Devi Temple, located atop Bilwa Parvat, offering panoramic views of the city. Explore the Chandi Devi Temple, accessible by a cable car ride, and seek blessings at this sacred shrine dedicated to Goddess Chandi. Later, visit other attractions like Maya Devi Temple, Bharat Mata Mandir, and Daksheshwar Mahadev Temple. In the evening, you can take a dip in the holy waters of the Ganges at Har Ki Pauri. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day 3:Haridwar to Rishikesh

After breakfast, proceed to Rishikesh, the "Yoga Capital of the World" and a spiritual hub nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and relax. In the afternoon, visit the iconic Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula, two suspension bridges over the Ganges River. Explore the bustling market area and visit the various ashrams and temples along the riverbanks. In the evening, attend the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony at Parmarth Niketan or Triveni Ghat. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Rishikesh.

Day 4:Rishikesh Adventure

After breakfast, embark on an adventurous day in Rishikesh. Participate in thrilling activities like white water rafting in the Ganges River, bungee jumping, or cliff jumping (optional and at your own expense). Visit Neer Garh Waterfall for a refreshing dip amidst nature's tranquility. You can also explore the nearby Beatles Ashram, also known as Maharishi Mahesh Yogi Ashram, famous for its graffiti art and serene ambiance. In the evening, relax and rejuvenate with a yoga or meditation session at one of the ashrams. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Rishikesh.

Day 5:Departure from Rishikesh to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Haridwar and Rishikesh as you proceed to Delhi/Chandigarh for your return journey. Depart with rejuvenated spirits and cherished memories of your spiritual and adventurous exploration of these sacred cities nestled in the lap of the Himalayas.
