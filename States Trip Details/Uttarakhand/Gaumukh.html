Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Gangotri

Your adventurous journey to the source of the Ganges begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Gangotri, the holy town nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. Anticipate the awe-inspiring landscapes and spiritual experiences that await you in the morning.

Day 2:Arrival in Gangotri and Acclimatization

Arrive in Gangotri in the morning, greeted by the pristine beauty of the Himalayan peaks and the sacred ambiance of the town. Check-in at your accommodation and take some time to rest and acclimatize to the altitude. Explore the enchanting surroundings of Gangotri, including the revered Gangotri Temple dedicated to Goddess Ganga. Spend the day soaking in the spiritual vibes and preparing for the trek ahead. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Gangotri.

Day 3:Trek from Gangotri to Bhojbasa

After breakfast, commence your trek towards Bhojbasa, the first campsite on the route to Gaumukh. Trek alongside the roaring Bhagirathi River, surrounded by majestic peaks and lush greenery. Traverse through rugged terrain and dense forests, enjoying the crisp mountain air and panoramic vistas. Arrive at Bhojbasa by afternoon and set up camp amidst the serene alpine meadows. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite.

Day 4:Trek from Bhojbasa to Gaumukh and Tapovan

Today marks the highlight of your trek as you journey to the sacred source of the Ganges - Gaumukh. After an early breakfast, embark on the trek to Gaumukh, a glacier resembling the mouth of a cow, from where the Ganges River originates. Marvel at the breathtaking views of the glacier and the surrounding peaks as you pay homage to this sacred pilgrimage site. After spending some time at Gaumukh, continue your trek to Tapovan, a high-altitude meadow offering panoramic views of the Bhagirathi peaks. Set up camp at Tapovan and revel in the tranquility of the Himalayan landscape. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite.

Day 5:Exploration and Rest Day at Tapovan

Today is reserved for exploration and relaxation at Tapovan. Wake up to majestic views of the surrounding peaks and spend the day exploring the enchanting meadows and glacial streams of Tapovan. You can indulge in meditation, yoga, or simply soak in the serene ambiance of this pristine Himalayan paradise. Take this opportunity to recharge your energies and immerse yourself in the spiritual aura of the mountains. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite in Tapovan.

Day 6:Descent from Tapovan to Gangotri

After breakfast, bid farewell to the tranquil surroundings of Tapovan as you begin your descent towards Gangotri. Retrace your steps through the picturesque trails, relishing the memories of your trek to the source of the Ganges. Arrive in Gangotri by afternoon and take some time to rest and rejuvenate. Celebrate the successful completion of your trek with your fellow trekkers, reminiscing about the unforgettable experiences shared along the way. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Gangotri.

Day 7:Departure from Gangotri to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid adieu to the majestic Himalayas and commence your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the spiritual and adventurous experiences gained during your trek to the source of the Ganges, carrying with you the blessings of the sacred river and the memories of the pristine Himalayan landscapes. Depart with a heart full of gratitude and reverence for the divine beauty of nature.
