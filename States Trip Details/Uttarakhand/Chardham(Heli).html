Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kharsali (Yamunotri)

Your Chardham pilgrimage via helicopter begins with a morning departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated helicopter and embark on the scenic flight to Kharsali, the base for Yamunotri, the first destination of the sacred journey. Prepare for a comfortable and swift aerial journey filled with anticipation and divine blessings.
Upon arrival in Kharsali, disembark from the helicopter and proceed to the helipad. From there, take a short trek or a palki ride to the Yamunotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Yamuna. Pay homage at the temple and take a holy dip in the hot springs of the Surya Kund. After offering prayers, return to Kharsali and check into your accommodation for the night.

Day 2:Visit to Gangotri

After a refreshing night in Kharsali, board the helicopter for your next destination, Gangotri, the origin of the sacred Ganges River. Enjoy a scenic flight over the Himalayas as you approach Gangotri. Upon arrival, visit the Gangotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Ganga, and offer your prayers at this revered shrine. Take a dip in the icy waters of the Bhagirathi River and experience the divine ambiance of Gangotri. Later, return to the helipad and proceed to your next destination.

Day 3:Journey to Kedarnath

Continue your divine journey as you fly towards Kedarnath, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a significant pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva. Upon arrival, visit the ancient Kedarnath Temple, nestled amidst the snow-capped Himalayas. Offer your prayers at the temple and immerse yourself in the spiritual vibes of this sacred sanctuary. Spend some time exploring the mystical surroundings of Kedarnath before returning to the helipad.

Day 4:Darshan at Badrinath

Conclude your Chardham pilgrimage with a visit to Badrinath, the abode of Lord Vishnu. Enjoy a scenic helicopter flight to Badrinath and land near the Badrinath Temple. Proceed for the darshan of the deity and explore the iconic temple complex. Visit nearby attractions such as the Mana Village, the last inhabited village before the Indo-Tibetan border, and the Vyas Cave. After completing the darshan and exploration, return to the helipad for your journey back.

Day 5:Return to Delhi/Chandigarh

After completing the divine journey to all four sacred destinations, bid farewell to the holy abodes and board the helicopter for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the sacred experiences and divine blessings received during your Chardham Yatra via helicopter. Depart with a heart filled with gratitude and reverence for the spiritual journey undertaken in the divine realm of Uttarakhand.
