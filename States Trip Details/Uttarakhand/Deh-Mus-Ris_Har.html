Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Dehradun

Your journey commences in the night from Delhi/Chandigarh as you board your designated transport heading towards Dehradun. Settle in for a comfortable overnight journey, anticipating the adventure and serenity awaiting you in the morning.

Day 2:Arrival in Dehradun and Sightseeing

You arrive in Dehradun in the morning, feeling refreshed and energized. Check-in at your hotel and freshen up before starting your day of exploration. Begin by visiting Robber's Cave, an intriguing natural formation perfect for adventure enthusiasts. Next, pay homage at the Tapkeshwar Temple, situated within a cave and dedicated to Lord Shiva. Conclude your day with a visit to the Forest Research Institute (FRI) Museum, showcasing the region's rich biodiversity. After a fulfilling day, return to your hotel for dinner and a restful overnight stay.

Day 3:Dehradun to Mussoorie

After breakfast, embark on a picturesque journey to Mussoorie, fondly known as the "Queen of Hills." Revel in the breathtaking views as you ascend winding roads enveloped in verdant greenery. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and take some time to relax. Later, explore the Mall Road, indulging in shopping and enjoying the charming ambiance. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Mussoorie.

Day 4:Mussoorie Exploration

Today is dedicated to exploring the beauty of Mussoorie. After breakfast, visit the majestic Kempty Falls and immerse yourself in the refreshing cascade of its waters. Continue your exploration with a visit to the serene Company Garden, offering a delightful retreat amidst colorful blooms. Conclude your day with a visit to Cloud's End, relishing panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes. Return to your hotel for dinner and a peaceful overnight stay.

Day 5:Mussoorie to Rishikesh

Depart from Mussoorie in the morning and journey towards Rishikesh, the "Yoga Capital of the World." En route, stop by Haridwar and witness the enchanting Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri ghat. Continue your journey to Rishikesh, arriving by morning. Check-in at your hotel and take some time to relax. In the evening, explore the local markets and riverside cafes at your leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Rishikesh.

Day 6:Rishikesh Adventures

Gear up for an exhilarating day of adventure in Rishikesh. After breakfast, partake in thrilling activities like white water rafting in the Ganges River, offering an adrenaline-pumping experience amidst stunning natural surroundings. Optional activities such as bungee jumping, zip-lining, or cliff jumping can also be enjoyed (at your own expense). In the evening, witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony at Triveni Ghat, offering a spiritually enriching experience. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Rishikesh.

Day 7:Departure from Rishikesh to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid adieu to the tranquil environs of Rishikesh as you depart for Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the unforgettable memories and experiences garnered during your journey as you make your way back home, carrying with you the essence of spirituality and adventure from your Uttarakhand expedition.
