Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Joshimath

Your Kuari Pass trek adventure begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Joshimath, a picturesque town nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. Prepare for an exhilarating trek through stunning landscapes and panoramic views of the Himalayan peaks.

Day 2:Arrival in Joshimath and Trek to Guling Top

Arrive in Joshimath in the morning and fuel up with a hearty breakfast. Meet your trekking team and begin the trek towards Guling Top, the starting point of the Kuari Pass trek. Trek through lush forests of oak and rhododendron, with the sound of chirping birds accompanying you along the way. As you gain altitude, soak in the breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and snow-capped peaks. Reach Guling Top by late afternoon and set up camp for the night. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Guling Top.

Day 3:Trek from Guling Top to Tali Top

After breakfast, resume your trek towards Tali Top, passing through enchanting meadows and alpine forests. Marvel at the panoramic vistas of Nanda Devi, Dronagiri, and other Himalayan peaks as you ascend higher into the mountains. En route, stop for a scenic lunch amidst the serene surroundings of the Himalayan wilderness. Arrive at Tali Top by late afternoon and set up camp for the night. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Tali Top.

Day 4:Trek from Tali Top to Khullara and Kuari Pass

Today, embark on the exhilarating trek to Khullara and Kuari Pass, the highest point of the trek offering breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks. Begin your ascent towards Khullara, trekking through rocky terrain and alpine meadows. As you approach Kuari Pass, the landscape becomes more rugged, with snow-capped peaks looming in the distance. Reach Kuari Pass by midday and celebrate your achievement with awe-inspiring views of the Himalayas. Spend some time soaking in the beauty of the surroundings before descending to Khullara. Dinner and overnight stay in tents at Khullara.

Day 5:Trek from Khullara to Auli and Departure to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, begin your descent from Khullara to Auli, a picturesque hill station renowned for its skiing slopes and lush meadows. Trek through pine forests and verdant valleys, with occasional glimpses of the towering Himalayan peaks. Upon reaching Auli, bid farewell to the majestic mountains and board your transport for the journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with a sense of accomplishment and a treasure trove of memories from your unforgettable Kuari Pass trek adventure.
