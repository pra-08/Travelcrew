Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Joshimath

Your journey to the mesmerizing Valley of Flowers begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Joshimath, a scenic town in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand. Prepare for an adventure filled with natural beauty and vibrant floral landscapes.

Day 2:Arrival in Joshimath and Transfer to Govindghat

Arrive in Joshimath in the morning and proceed to Govindghat, the starting point of the trek to the Valley of Flowers. Upon reaching Govindghat, check into your accommodation and take some time to rest and acclimatize to the altitude. Spend the rest of the day exploring the quaint surroundings of Govindghat or visiting the nearby attractions such as the Hemkund Sahib Gurudwara. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Govindghat.

Day 3:Trek from Govindghat to Ghangaria

After an early breakfast, commence your trek from Govindghat to Ghangaria, the base camp for the Valley of Flowers. Trek alongside the gushing Alaknanda River, traversing through picturesque forests and verdant meadows adorned with colorful blooms. The trek to Ghangaria is moderate and offers stunning views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks. Arrive in Ghangaria by afternoon and check into your accommodation. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Ghangaria.

Day 4:Trek to the Valley of Flowers

Today marks the highlight of your journey as you trek to the enchanting Valley of Flowers. After breakfast, set out on the trail surrounded by a kaleidoscope of vibrant flowers blooming in their full glory. Marvel at the diversity of flora, including rare Himalayan flowers such as the blue poppy and the brahma kamal. Spend the day exploring the valley, capturing the beauty of the landscape and immersing yourself in its serene ambiance. Return to Ghangaria by late afternoon. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Ghangaria.

Day 5:Trek to Hemkund Sahib and Return to Govindghat

After an early breakfast, embark on a trek to Hemkund Sahib, one of the highest gurudwaras in the world, situated at an altitude of over 15,000 feet. The trek to Hemkund Sahib is challenging but rewarding, offering panoramic views of the surrounding peaks and the crystal-clear waters of the lake. Pay your respects at the gurudwara and take some time to soak in the spiritual vibes of this sacred site. Afterward, trek back to Govindghat. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Govindghat.

Day 6:Departure from Govindghat to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the picturesque landscapes of Govindghat as you commence your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the unforgettable experiences and soul-stirring moments spent amidst the natural beauty of the Valley of Flowers. Depart with a heart full of gratitude for the rejuvenating retreat and the memories of the vibrant floral paradise.
