Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Chopta

Your adventure to Chopta Tungnath begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Chopta, a scenic hamlet nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. Look forward to waking up amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Uttarakhand.

Day 2:Arrival in Chopta and Trek to Tungnath

Arrive in Chopta in the morning and check into your accommodation. After a refreshing breakfast, begin your trek to Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world. The trek to Tungnath is a moderate one, offering mesmerizing views of the surrounding peaks and lush greenery. Upon reaching Tungnath, visit the ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and soak in the serene ambiance of the Himalayas. Later, continue your trek to Chandrashila Peak, offering panoramic views of the snow-capped mountains. After exploring the area, descend back to Chopta. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Chopta.

Day 3:Exploration of Chopta and Surroundings

After breakfast, spend the day exploring the natural beauty and serene surroundings of Chopta. You can take leisurely walks amidst the pine forests, visit the nearby Deoria Tal for a scenic view of the Himalayas reflected in its crystal-clear waters, or simply relax amidst the tranquil ambiance of the mountains. In the evening, witness the mesmerizing sunset painting the sky in hues of orange and pink. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Chopta.

Day 4:Trek to Kartik Swami Temple and Return to Chopta

After breakfast, embark on a trek to Kartik Swami Temple, dedicated to Lord Kartikeya, the son of Lord Shiva. The trek offers panoramic views of the Himalayas and the picturesque landscape of Uttarakhand. Upon reaching the temple, offer your prayers and soak in the spiritual vibes of this sacred site. After spending some time at Kartik Swami Temple, trek back to Chopta. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Chopta.

Day 5:Departure from Chopta to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the enchanting landscapes of Chopta as you commence your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Reflect on the memorable experiences and soul-stirring moments spent amidst the Himalayan wilderness. Depart with a heart full of gratitude for the rejuvenating retreat and the blessings of the majestic mountains.
