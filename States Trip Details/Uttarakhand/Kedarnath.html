Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Haridwar

Your journey to Kedarnath begins with your departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Haridwar. Board your designated transport and embark on a scenic drive towards the holy city of Haridwar, situated on the banks of the Ganges River. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and take some time to relax. You can visit the famous Har Ki Pauri ghat in the evening to witness the Ganga Aarti, a mesmerizing ritual filled with lights, music, and prayers. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day 2:Haridwar to Guptkashi

After breakfast, proceed towards Guptkashi, a small town located on the route to Kedarnath. The journey offers picturesque views of the Himalayan ranges and the Mandakini River. Upon arrival in Guptkashi, check-in at your hotel and relax. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the serene surroundings or visiting the local temples. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 3:Guptkashi to Kedarnath

After an early breakfast, begin your journey towards Kedarnath, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and an important pilgrimage site for Hindus. You can reach Kedarnath by trekking from Gaurikund or by hiring a helicopter service (subject to availability). Upon arrival in Kedarnath, check-in at your accommodation and proceed to visit the Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Seek the blessings of Lord Kedarnath and spend some time exploring the tranquil surroundings. Dinner and overnight stay at the accommodation in Kedarnath.

Day 4:Kedarnath to Haridwar

After breakfast, bid farewell to the sacred land of Kedarnath and begin your journey back to Haridwar. You can choose to trek down to Gaurikund or avail of the helicopter service (subject to availability). Upon reaching Haridwar, check-in at your hotel and relax. Spend the evening at leisure, reflecting on your spiritual journey and exploring the local markets. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day 5:Departure from Haridwar

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Haridwar railway station or airport for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with divine blessings and cherished memories of your pilgrimage to Kedarnath, filled with spiritual experiences and breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
