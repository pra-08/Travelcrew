Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Nainital

Embark on your journey to Nainital, a picturesque hill station in Uttarakhand, from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and enjoy a scenic drive to Nainital. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and unwind after the journey. Spend the evening leisurely exploring the Mall Road and enjoying a serene boat ride on Naini Lake. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Nainital.

Day 2:Nainital Sightseeing

After breakfast, set out to explore the beauty of Nainital. Visit the Naina Devi Temple, perched atop the Naina Hill, offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and the town. Proceed to Snow View Point for breathtaking vistas of the Himalayas. Later, visit the Tiffin Top and Eco Cave Gardens for a blend of nature and adventure. In the evening, stroll along the Mall Road, indulging in shopping and trying local delicacies. Dinner and overnight stay in Nainital.

Day 3:Nainital to Almora

After breakfast, depart for Almora, a serene hill town known for its cultural heritage and panoramic views of the Himalayas. En route, visit Jageshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. Seek blessings at this ancient temple complex surrounded by deodar forests. Continue your journey to Almora and upon arrival, check-in at your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the charming streets and enjoying the serene ambiance of Almora. Dinner and overnight stay in Almora.

Day 4:Almora Exploration

After breakfast, set out to explore the beauty and tranquility of Almora. Visit Kasar Devi Temple, known for its spiritual significance and stunning views of the Himalayas. Explore Bright End Corner, a popular vantage point offering panoramic views of sunrise and sunset. You can also visit Chitai Golu Devta Temple, known for its unique architecture and cultural importance. Spend the evening at leisure, immersing yourself in the serene surroundings of Almora. Dinner and overnight stay in Almora.

Day 5:Departure from Almora to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Nainital and Almora as you embark on your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with cherished memories of your trip, filled with spiritual experiences, natural beauty, and cultural exploration of these enchanting hill stations.
