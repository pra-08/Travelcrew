Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Sankri

Your adventurous journey to Kedarkantha begins with a night departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the overnight journey to Sankri, the base camp for the Kedarkantha trek. Anticipate the thrilling experiences and breathtaking vistas that await you in the morning.

Day 2:Arrival in Sankri and Trek to Juda-ka-Talab

Arrive in Sankri in the morning, welcomed by the serene beauty of the Himalayan village. After a hearty breakfast, commence your trek towards Juda-ka-Talab, the first campsite on the Kedarkantha trail. Trek through dense forests of pine and oak trees, enjoying the crisp mountain air and panoramic views of snow-capped peaks. Reach Juda-ka-Talab by afternoon and set up camp amidst the tranquil surroundings of the alpine lake. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite.

Day 3:Trek from Juda-ka-Talab to Kedarkantha Base Camp

After breakfast, resume your trek towards the Kedarkantha Base Camp, ascending through meadows adorned with vibrant alpine flowers. Marvel at the majestic views of Swargarohini and Bandarpoonch peaks as you make your way towards higher altitudes. Reach the base camp, situated at an altitude of approximately 11,250 feet, by late afternoon. Settle into your campsite and soak in the breathtaking vistas of the surrounding Himalayan ranges. Dinner and overnight stay at the base camp.

Day 4:Summit Day - Kedarkantha Peak and Descend to Hargaon

Wake up before dawn and embark on the final ascent to the summit of Kedarkantha Peak, standing tall at an elevation of 12,500 feet. Traverse through snow-covered trails, witnessing the ethereal beauty of the Himalayan landscape bathed in the soft morning light. Reach the summit and be rewarded with panoramic views of the surrounding peaks, including Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, and the Gangotri range. After spending some time at the summit, begin your descent towards Hargaon, the next campsite on the trail. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite in Hargaon.

Day 5:Descent from Hargaon to Sankri and Departure

After breakfast, commence your descent from Hargaon to Sankri, retracing your steps through the picturesque forests and meadows. Arrive in Sankri by afternoon and bid farewell to the tranquil mountains that have been your home for the past few days. Board your transport and embark on the journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh, filled with memories of adventure and the splendor of the Himalayas.
