Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi

Your journey to the pristine islands of Lakshadweep begins with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi, the gateway to the Lakshadweep archipelago. Upon arrival in Kochi, you will be transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2:Kochi to Agatti Island

After breakfast, transfer to the Kochi Airport for your flight to Agatti Island, the entry point to the Lakshadweep Islands. Upon arrival at Agatti Airport, you will be transferred to your resort. Spend the day relaxing on the pristine beaches or indulge in water sports activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking. Overnight stay at Agatti Island.

Day 3:Agatti Island - Bangaram Island

After breakfast, embark on a scenic boat journey to Bangaram Island, one of the most beautiful islands in Lakshadweep. Check into your beach resort and spend the day exploring the island's stunning beaches, coral reefs, and turquoise lagoons. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters. Overnight stay at Bangaram Island.

Day 4:Bangaram Island - Thinnakara Island - Parali Island

Today, set out on an island-hopping adventure to Thinnakara Island and Parali Island. Explore the pristine beaches, coconut groves, and coral reefs of Thinnakara, known for its untouched natural beauty. Continue your journey to Parali Island, where you can relax on the secluded beaches and enjoy the tranquility of the surroundings. Overnight stay at Bangaram Island.

Day 5:Bangaram Island - Kadmat Island

After breakfast, depart for Kadmat Island, known for its long sandy beaches and vibrant marine life. Check into your beach resort and spend the day exploring the island's coral reefs, snorkeling spots, and water sports activities. Enjoy leisurely walks on the beach and witness a mesmerizing sunset over the Arabian Sea. Overnight stay at Kadmat Island.

Day 6:Kadmat Island - Agatti Island

Today, return to Agatti Island by boat. Upon arrival, check into your resort and spend the day relaxing on the beach or exploring the island's attractions. Visit the Agatti Beach, known for its turquoise waters and white sandy shores. Alternatively, you can indulge in water sports activities such as Windsurfing, kayaking, and sailing. Overnight stay at Agatti Island.

Day 7:Agatti Island - Kochi

After breakfast, bid farewell to the beautiful islands of Lakshadweep as you board your flight back to Kochi. Upon arrival in Kochi, you will be transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 8:Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, depending on your flight schedule, you may have some free time to explore Kochi's attractions, such as the Fort Kochi area, Chinese Fishing Nets, and Mattancherry Palace. Later, transfer to the Kochi Airport for your onward journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh, carrying with you unforgettable memories of your Lakshadweep adventure.
