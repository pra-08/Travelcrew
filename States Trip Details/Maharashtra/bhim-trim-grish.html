Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Bhimashankar

Embark on a spiritual journey from Delhi/Chandigarh to the sacred town of Bhimashankar, renowned for the revered Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga. Board the designated transport and begin a scenic drive through the picturesque landscapes. Enjoy the serene views as you traverse towards your destination. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel and unwind. Spend the evening exploring the serene surroundings or visiting the Bhimashankar Temple to witness the evening aarti. Dinner and overnight stay in Bhimashankar.

Day 2:Bhimashankar Darshan and Trek

After an early breakfast, visit the Bhimashankar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Seek blessings and partake in the morning rituals. Later, embark on an exhilarating trek through the lush forests of the Sahyadri range to explore the Bhimashankar Wildlife Sanctuary. Marvel at the diverse flora and fauna as you trek to the Hanuman Lake and Gupt Bhimashankar. Enjoy a packed lunch amidst nature's tranquility. Return to the hotel in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3:Bhimashankar to Trimbakeshwar

After breakfast, proceed to Trimbakeshwar, home to the Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga, located near the holy river Godavari in Nashik district. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Visit the Trimbakeshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and participate in the sacred rituals. Explore the temple complex and admire its architectural grandeur. Spend the evening exploring the local markets or relaxing by the riverbank. Dinner and overnight stay in Trimbakeshwar.

Day 4:Trimbakeshwar to Grishneshwar

After breakfast, depart for Grishneshwar, the abode of the Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga, located near Ellora Caves in Aurangabad district. En route, visit the famous Shirdi Sai Baba Temple and seek blessings. Continue your journey to Grishneshwar and check-in at the hotel upon arrival. Visit the Grishneshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and immerse yourself in spirituality. Explore the nearby Ellora Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures. Dinner and overnight stay in Grishneshwar.

Day 5:Departure from Grishneshwar to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the divine land of Grishneshwar as you begin your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with divine blessings and cherished memories of your spiritual journey through the sacred towns of Bhimashankar, Trimbakeshwar, and Grishneshwar.
