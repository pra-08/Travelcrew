Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Bagdogra

Your journey to the enchanting land of Sikkim begins with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Bagdogra. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to Gangtok, the capital city of Sikkim. Check-in at your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure, acclimatizing to the high altitude. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 2:Gangtok Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a full-day sightseeing tour of Gangtok. Visit the iconic Rumtek Monastery, one of the largest and most significant monasteries in Sikkim. Explore the Enchey Monastery, known for its serene ambiance and stunning architecture. Proceed to the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology to learn about Tibetan culture and history. Later, visit the Flower Exhibition Centre and enjoy a stroll through the vibrant flower gardens. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 3:Gangtok to Lachen

After breakfast, depart for Lachen, a picturesque mountain village nestled in the Himalayas. En route, visit the Seven Sisters Waterfall and Singhik Viewpoint for panoramic views of the snow-capped mountains. Upon arrival in Lachen, check into your hotel and relax. Spend the evening exploring the local markets and interacting with the friendly locals. Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 4:Lachen to Gurudongmar Lake Excursion

Today, embark on an unforgettable excursion to Gurudongmar Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world located at an altitude of over 17,000 feet. Marvel at the pristine beauty of the lake surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Offer prayers at the sacred Gurudwara near the lake and soak in the spiritual ambiance. Later, return to Lachen and spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Lachen.

Day 5:Lachen to Lachung

After breakfast, proceed to Lachung, another charming mountain village known for its natural beauty and warm hospitality. En route, visit the picturesque Chopta Valley and Thangu Valley, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayan landscape. Upon arrival in Lachung, check into your hotel and relax. In the evening, explore the local surroundings and interact with the villagers. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 6:Lachung to Yumthang Valley Excursion

Today, embark on an excursion to the scenic Yumthang Valley, also known as the Valley of Flowers. Marvel at the colorful alpine flowers, meandering streams, and snow-capped peaks. Visit the Yumthang Hot Springs and enjoy a relaxing soak in the natural hot springs amidst the tranquil surroundings. Later, return to Lachung and spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Lachung.

Day 7:Lachung to Gangtok

After breakfast, bid farewell to the picturesque landscapes of Lachung as you drive back to Gangtok. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local markets or indulging in optional activities such as river rafting or paragliding (subject to availability). Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 8:Departure from Gangtok to Bagdogra

After breakfast, depending on your flight schedule, you may have some free time to explore Gangtok further or indulge in last-minute shopping. Later, transfer to Bagdogra Airport for your onward journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh, carrying with you fond memories of your unforgettable trip to Sikkim.
