Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh->Shimla

Start your wonderful journey to Shimla by boarding the Volvo from Chandigarh/Delhi Volvo pick-up point in the night. The location of pickup point will be shared, Guests are requested to report at the scheduled time.


As soon as you enter the terrains of Shimla, you will feel a cool breeze already taking all your stress away and surprising you with its jaw-dropping landscapes.

Day 2:Exploring Shimla

You will reach Shimla in the morning and will be given few hours check-in at the hotel,have Breakfast and take some rest. You will be exploring various places like – Christ Church, Hanuman Mandir Jakhoo, Jakhoo Hill, Jakhoo Ropeway, Scandal Point.

After you explore these places, in the evening visit “Mall Road” and return to the hotel, have dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Dalhousie.

Day 3:Exploring Kufri

The next day we will head toward Kufri. From Kufri we will be heading to Makhrol on our Horses.


Kufri Adventure park is located in Makhrol which includes sight seeing with telescope(places like- Temple built by Pandavas, India-China Border, Palace that turned into 5-Star Hotel), Adventure includes Zipline, Zip-Climbing, Freefall, Rope Bridge, Rope-Climing. We will return to hotel and have dinner and take rest.

Day 4:Exploring Chail

Early in the morning after breakfast we will head towards Chail and we eill b staying in Camp at Chail Forest provided with Camp Fire. After settling our camps we will be visiting places like - Chail Palace, Stoned Kumbh Shiv Temple, Kali Tibba Temple and Chail Sanctuary.


We will return to our Camps in the Evening by 7-8 and the dinner is planned with camp fire and music.

Day 5:Sweet Home

Early morning we will go for a small trek at 7 and after returning we will have our breakfast and head towards our sweet home via Volvo.
