Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kasol

Begin your adventure to Kasol, starting with a departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the scenic journey to Kasol, nestled in the Parvati Valley. Enjoy the picturesque landscapes and lush greenery along the way. Upon arrival in Kasol, check into your accommodation and unwind after the journey. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant streets of Kasol and savoring the local cuisine. Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 2:Trek to Tosh

After breakfast, gear up for a trek to Tosh, a quaint village nestled amidst the mountains. Trek through scenic trails surrounded by pine forests and meadows dotted with wildflowers. As you ascend, enjoy panoramic views of the Parvati Valley. Reach Tosh and explore the serene surroundings. Visit the Tosh Temple and soak in the peaceful ambiance of the village. Overnight stay in Tosh.

Day 3:Visit to Manikaran

Today, embark on a journey to Manikaran, renowned for its hot springs and religious significance. Explore the revered Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara, where devotees take a dip in the hot springs for spiritual cleansing. Visit the Ram Temple and explore the local market, known for its handicrafts and souvenirs. Later, return to Kasol and spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 4:Leisure Day in Kasol

Enjoy a leisurely day in Kasol, exploring its charming cafes, serene riverbanks, and scenic trails. Indulge in adventure activities like trekking, river rafting, or simply unwind amidst the tranquil surroundings. Visit the Kasol Market and shop for souvenirs and local handicrafts. In the evening, relax by the bonfire and relish the beauty of the starlit sky. Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 5:Departure from Kasol to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the mesmerizing landscapes of Kasol and begin your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Carry with you fond memories of your adventurous retreat in the heart of the Himalayas, promising to return for more exploration in the future.
