Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh->Manali

Start your wonderful journey to Manali by boarding the Volvo from Chandigarh/Delhi Volvo pick-up point in the night. The location of pickup point will be shared, Guests are requested to report at the scheduled time.


As soon as you enter the terrains of Manali, you will feel a cool breeze already taking all your stress away and surprising you with its jaw-dropping landscapes.

Day 2:Manali (Local Sight-seeing)

You will reach Manali in the morning and will be given few hours check-in at the hotel,have Breakfast and take some rest. You will be exploring various places like – "Hadimba Temple" built in 1553 with a superbly crafted pagoda roof, it is famous for its exquisitely carved doorway. "Club House" with comprehensive facilities that include roller skating, an auditorium, a billiards room, a library, a bar, and a restaurant makes a wonderful outing for the day.

After you explore these places, in the evening visit “Mall Road” and return to the hotel, have dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 3:Solang Valley & Atal Tunnel

The morning after breakfast, the tourists can proceed from their hotel to the- “Solang Valley”. Here they can enjoy various adventure activities such as Zorbing, Snow scooter, Horse Riding, Paragliding, Skiing, and a lot more. And also can enjoy a short Trek to “Anjani Mahadev”.
The tourists also go to the - “Rohtang Pass” that is near only and experience the snow-capped mountains. Go through “Atal Tunnel” and also visit Kothi Village, Nehru Kund, Him Valley, and even Gulaba.

Please Note: Since the National Green Tribunal is allowing only limited vehicles to Rohtang pass per day. Rohtang pass will be subject to the availability of a permit on the spot with additional cost on sharing basis or individual basis as per guest requirement & Rohtang is closed every Tuesday.

Day 4:Kullu-Kasol-Manikaran

On the next day, the morning after breakfast, tourists can visit the Pilgrimage – Manikaran Sahib Gurdwara-(Natural Hot Spring Water) and enjoy the hot water springs that have medical properties & take the blessings of God/goodness. The tourists can explore the nearby places like -- Kasol, Kasol Market, Shiv Temple, Flea Market, Parvati Valley, and they can even do trekking.

Tourists also visit :- Kullu, Vaishno Devi Temple, Kullu Market, Kullu Valley, Rabbit farm & "RIVER RAFTING" – River Rafting is also very famous around there and visit the Handicrafts and Shawls Factory. Later, return to the hotel and have your dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 5:Back to Manali

After Breakfast at the hotel, you need to vacate the room by 11 AM. The afternoon will be at leisure. You can explore the Shopping "Malls of Manali" on your own. At 05 PM board the Volvo AC bus for Delhi for an overnight journey. And tourists also visit -- "Tibetan Monastery", "Van Vihar", "Manu Temple", "Jogani Waterfall" and “Vashist Temple” are well known for their Hot springs. There are old temples dedicated to the Saga Vashist and Lord Rama.

Drop from the Hotel to Manali Bus Stand by Taxi will be provided by us. Board your Volvo to get back to your hometown. For the departure as well the couples can book from the customizable options that are available in the package. The hotels, food, sports, and destinations are according to their choice and preferences.

Day 6:Sweet Home

On your 6th day, bid farewell to Manali ,far the tourists will reach Delhi/Chnadigarh by 8 am in the morning.
