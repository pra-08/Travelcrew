Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh to Dharamshala

Commence your journey from Delhi/Chandigarh towards the serene town of Dharamshala. Board the Volvo from the designated pick-up point and enjoy a comfortable overnight journey.

Day 2:Dharamshala Exploration

Arrive in Dharamshala in the morning and check-in at the hotel. After freshening up, start your exploration of this tranquil town. Visit the Namgyal Monastery, also known as the Dalai Lama Temple, to experience the peaceful ambiance and witness Buddhist rituals. Explore the vibrant streets of McLeod Ganj, the Tibetan settlement, and indulge in delicious Tibetan cuisine. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Dharamshala.

Day 3:Triund Trek

Wake up early and prepare for an adventurous trek to Triund, a moderate trek of 9 Km. Begin your trek from McLeod Ganj, passing through scenic trails surrounded by lush greenery and panoramic views of the Dhauladhar Range. Reach the top of Triund and marvel at the breathtaking vistas of the Himalayas. Spend the night camping under the starlit sky, enjoying a bonfire and delicious dinner amidst nature's embrace.

Day 4:McLeod Ganj Sightseeing

After breakfast at Triund, trek back to McLeod Ganj and return to your hotel in Dharamshala for some relaxation. Later in the day, visit the Bhagsunag Temple and Bhagsu Waterfall, where you can take a refreshing dip in the natural pool. Explore the Tibetan Museum to learn about the rich Tibetan culture and history. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Dharamshala.

Day 5:Kareri Lake Trek

Embark on another thrilling trek, this time to Kareri Lake, a pristine alpine lake nestled amidst the Dhauladhar Range. Start the trek from Kareri village, passing through dense forests, meadows, and gushing streams. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the surroundings and the panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks. Reach Kareri Lake and spend the night camping by the tranquil waters, surrounded by nature's serenity.

Day 6:Palampur Excursion

After breakfast at Kareri Lake, trek back to Kareri village and proceed to Palampur, known for its tea gardens and scenic beauty. Visit the famous tea estates and learn about the tea-making process during a guided tour. Explore the Tashi Jong Monastery, known for its intricate Tibetan architecture and serene ambiance. Enjoy a leisurely walk amidst the lush greenery of Palampur and soak in the tranquility of nature. Return to Dharamshala in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 7:Departure from Dharamshala

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Dharamshala as you head back to Delhi/Chandigarh via Volvo. Depart with cherished memories of your journey through the enchanting destinations of Dharamshala, McLeod Ganj, Triund, Kareri, and Palampur.
