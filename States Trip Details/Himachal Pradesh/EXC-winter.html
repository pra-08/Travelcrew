Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Shimla

Your exclusive winter journey to Spiti begins with a departure from Delhi/Chandigarh. Board your designated transport and embark on the scenic drive to Shimla, the capital city of Himachal Pradesh. Enjoy the picturesque landscapes and snow-capped mountains along the way. Upon arrival in Shimla, check into your accommodation and unwind after the journey. Spend the evening exploring the charming Mall Road and indulging in local delicacies. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2:Shimla to Narkanda

After breakfast, depart from Shimla and drive to Narkanda, a serene hill station known for its apple orchards and snow-covered peaks. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the Himalayas as you ascend higher into the mountains. Upon reaching Narkanda, check into your accommodation and relax amidst the tranquil surroundings. Spend the day exploring the quaint village and indulging in activities like skiing and snowboarding (subject to availability). Overnight stay in Narkanda.

Day 3:Narkanda to Kalpa

Today, embark on a scenic drive from Narkanda to Kalpa, a picturesque village located in the Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh. Marvel at the stunning views of the Kinner Kailash range and the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas. Upon arrival in Kalpa, check into your accommodation and soak in the serene ambiance of the village. Explore the ancient monasteries and traditional Himachali architecture of Kalpa. Overnight stay in Kalpa.

Day 4:Kalpa to Tabo

After breakfast, continue your journey towards Tabo, a historic town famous for its ancient monasteries and Tibetan culture. Drive through rugged terrain and barren landscapes, marveling at the dramatic scenery of the Spiti Valley. Upon reaching Tabo, visit the renowned Tabo Monastery, also known as the "Ajanta of the Himalayas," and explore its exquisite frescoes and ancient relics. Overnight stay in Tabo.

Day 5:Tabo to Kaza

Today, proceed from Tabo to Kaza, the administrative headquarters of the Spiti Valley. Enjoy a scenic drive through winding mountain roads and picturesque villages, with the majestic Himalayas as your backdrop. Upon arrival in Kaza, check into your accommodation and relax after the journey. Spend the evening exploring the charming town of Kaza and visiting the local markets. Overnight stay in Kaza.

Day 6:Exploration of Key Monastery and Kibber Village

After breakfast, embark on an excursion to Key Monastery, one of the largest and oldest monasteries in Spiti. Explore the monastery complex and admire its stunning architecture and ancient Buddhist artifacts. Continue your journey to Kibber Village, one of the highest inhabited villages in the world. Explore the traditional houses and interact with the friendly locals. In the evening, return to Kaza and enjoy a cozy dinner by the fireplace. Overnight stay in Kaza.

Day 7:Kaza to Chandratal Lake

Today, embark on a memorable journey to Chandratal Lake, also known as the "Moon Lake," located at an altitude of 4,300 meters above sea level. Enjoy a scenic drive through rugged terrain and pristine landscapes, with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Upon reaching Chandratal Lake, marvel at its crystal-clear waters and surreal beauty. Spend some time soaking in the tranquility of the lake and capturing breathtaking photographs. Overnight camping near Chandratal Lake.

Day 8:Chandratal Lake to Manali

After breakfast, bid farewell to Chandratal Lake and drive to Manali, a popular hill station nestled in the Kullu Valley. Enjoy a scenic drive through the picturesque Rohtang Pass, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green valleys. Upon arrival in Manali, check into your accommodation and relax after the journey. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant markets and bustling streets of Manali. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 9:Departure from Manali to Delhi/Chandigarh

After breakfast, bid farewell to the enchanting beauty of the Spiti Valley and begin your journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh. Carry with you fond memories of your exclusive winter retreat amidst the majestic Himalayas, promising to return for more adventures in the future.
