Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh->Dalhousie

Start your wonderful journey to Dalhousie by boarding the Volvo from Chandigarh/Delhi Volvo pick-up point in the night. The location of pickup point will be shared, Guests are requested to report at the scheduled time.


As soon as you enter the terrains of Dalhousie, you will feel a cool breeze already taking all your stress away and surprising you with its jaw-dropping landscapes.

Day 2:Dalhousie Sight Seeing

You will reach Dalhousie in the morning and will be given few hours check-in at the hotel,have Breakfast and take some rest. You will be exploring various places like – Daikund Peak, Chamera Lake, Ganji Pahadi Trekking.

After you explore these places, in the evening visit “Mall Road” and return to the hotel, have dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Dalhousie.

Day 3:Khajjiar Sight Seeing

The next day morning we head towards Khajjiar. We will be exploring places like - Khajjiar Lake, Lord Shiva Statue, Pholani Devi Temple, A horse ride to Chamba through dense Green forest.


We will return to hotel in Khajjiar near Khajjiar Lake and have dinner at roofless restaurant as per the weather supports.

Day 4:Sweet home

Early morning after witnessing the beautiful sunrise at khajjiar we will head towards our sweet home via Volvo.
