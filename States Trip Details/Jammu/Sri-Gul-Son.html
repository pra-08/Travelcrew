Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Srinagar

Your journey begins as you depart from Delhi/Chandigarh towards the picturesque valley of Srinagar. Board your designated transport and enjoy a scenic flight to Srinagar. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel and check-in. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local markets or taking a serene shikara ride on the iconic Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 2:Srinagar Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Srinagar. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens including Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, showcasing exquisite Mughal architecture and serene landscapes. Explore the historic Jama Masjid and marvel at its architectural grandeur. In the evening, take a leisurely walk along the scenic Boulevard Road and enjoy the breathtaking views of Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 3:Srinagar to Gulmarg

After breakfast, proceed towards Gulmarg, known as the "Meadow of Flowers." Enjoy a picturesque drive through the stunning Kashmir Valley. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and freshen up. Explore Gulmarg's natural beauty, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush meadows. Embark on the famous Gulmarg Gondola ride, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas. Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local markets or indulging in adventure activities like skiing and snowboarding (seasonal). Dinner and overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day 4:Gulmarg to Sonmarg

After breakfast, depart for Sonmarg, known as the "Meadow of Gold," nestled in the Sindh Valley. Enjoy a scenic drive through the picturesque landscapes of Kashmir. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and relax. Explore the breathtaking beauty of Sonmarg, surrounded by majestic mountains and glistening glaciers. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy activities like pony rides or trekking amidst nature's tranquility. Dinner and overnight stay in Sonmarg.

Day 5:Sonmarg Exploration and Return to Srinagar

After breakfast, spend the day exploring the scenic beauty of Sonmarg. Visit the enchanting Gangbal Lake, known for its pristine blue waters and stunning surroundings. Take a leisurely stroll along the banks of the Nallah Sindh River and immerse yourself in the serene ambiance. In the afternoon, return to Srinagar. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and relax. Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local markets or enjoying a relaxing shikara ride on Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 6:Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Kashmir as you transfer to the airport for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with cherished memories of your unforgettable trip, filled with scenic beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences in Srinagar, Gulmarg, and Sonmarg.
