Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Srinagar

Embark on your adventure from Delhi/Chandigarh to the enchanting valley of Srinagar. Board your designated transport and enjoy a scenic flight to Srinagar. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel and check-in. Spend the day at leisure, exploring the local markets or taking a serene shikara ride on the iconic Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 2:Srinagar to Gulmarg

After breakfast, journey towards Gulmarg, renowned as the "Meadow of Flowers." Enjoy a picturesque drive through the stunning Kashmir Valley. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and freshen up. Explore Gulmarg's natural beauty, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and verdant meadows. Embark on the famous Gulmarg Gondola ride, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas. Later, visit the historic Gulmarg Golf Course, one of the highest golf courses in the world. Dinner and overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day 3:Gulmarg to Sonmarg

After breakfast, depart for Sonmarg, known as the "Meadow of Gold," nestled in the Sindh Valley. En route, visit the charming town of Tangmarg and marvel at its scenic beauty. Upon arrival in Sonmarg, check-in at your hotel and relax. Explore the mesmerizing landscapes of Sonmarg, surrounded by lush green valleys and snow-capped peaks. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy a thrilling pony ride or trekking adventure. Dinner and overnight stay in Sonmarg.

Day 4:Sonmarg to Pahalgam

After breakfast, journey towards Pahalgam, a picturesque town situated along the Lidder River. En route, visit the captivating Betaab Valley, named after the Bollywood movie "Betaab" filmed here. Explore the scenic beauty of Pahalgam, adorned with dense pine forests and pristine meadows. Visit the famous Aru Valley and Chandanwari, known for their stunning natural beauty and adventure activities like trekking and river rafting. Dinner and overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 5:Pahalgam to Srinagar

After breakfast, return to Srinagar, the heart of the Kashmir Valley. En route, visit the iconic Mughal Gardens including Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, showcasing exquisite Mughal architecture and lush greenery. Upon arrival in Srinagar, check-in at your hotel and relax. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant local markets or taking a leisurely walk along the Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 6:Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Kashmir as you transfer to the airport for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with cherished memories of your unforgettable trip, filled with scenic beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences in Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonmarg, and Pahalgam.
