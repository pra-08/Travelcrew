Day 1:Arrival in Jammu

Your pilgrimage to Vaishno Devi begins with your departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Jammu. Board your designated transport and embark on a scenic journey towards the holy city of Jammu. Upon arrival, you'll be greeted and transferred to your hotel. Check-in and relax after the journey. You can spend the evening exploring the local markets or visiting nearby attractions. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Jammu.

Day 2:Transfer to Katra and Vaishno Devi Darshan

After an early breakfast, proceed to Katra, the base camp for the Vaishno Devi pilgrimage. Upon arrival in Katra, check-in at your hotel and prepare for the trek to the holy shrine of Vaishno Devi. The trek to the Vaishno Devi Temple is approximately 13 kilometers long and can be completed on foot, by pony, or by palanquin. Seek the blessings of Mata Vaishno Devi at the sacred cave shrine. After darshan at vaishno devi trek to Baba Bhairo Nath, a trek of 2 Km. After, begin your descent back to Katra. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Katra.

Day 3:Leisure Day in Katra

After breakfast, enjoy a leisure day in Katra. You can explore the local markets for souvenirs or relax at the hotel. Alternatively, you may choose to visit nearby attractions such as Baba Dhansar, Shiv Khori, or Ardh Kuwari Cave. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Katra.

Day 4:Departure from Jammu

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Jammu for your return journey. Depending on your departure schedule, you may have some time to explore more of Jammu or indulge in some last-minute shopping. Later, you'll be transferred to the airport or railway station for your journey back home, carrying divine blessings and cherished memories of your Vaishno Devi pilgrimage.
