Day 1: Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi

Your journey begins with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to Munnar, a charming hill station known for its tea plantations and picturesque landscapes. Check into your hotel in Munnar and spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Munnar. Visit popular attractions such as the Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Tea Museum. Explore the lush tea gardens and enjoy scenic views of the Western Ghats. Return to your hotel and relax. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar to Alleppey Houseboat

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey, known as the Venice of the East. Board your traditional houseboat and cruise through the enchanting backwaters of Alleppey. Marvel at the serene surroundings and enjoy delicious Kerala cuisine served onboard. Witness a mesmerizing sunset over the backwaters. Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 4: Alleppey to Kochi

Disembark from the houseboat after breakfast and drive to Kochi. Check into your hotel and relax for a while. In the afternoon, explore the cultural heritage of Kochi by visiting attractions like the Chinese Fishing Nets, Mattancherry Palace, and Jewish Synagogue. Enjoy a Kathakali dance performance in the evening. Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 5: Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, transfer to Kochi Airport for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with fond memories of your trip to Munnar, Alleppey, and Kochi.
