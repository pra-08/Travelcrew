Day 1: Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi

Your journey starts with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Kochi. Upon arrival, our representative will greet you and transfer you to Munnar, a delightful hill station renowned for its tea gardens and scenic vistas. Check into your Munnar hotel and unwind. Enjoy the evening at your leisure. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing excursion in Munnar. Visit the iconic Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and the Tea Museum to delve into the region's rich tea heritage. Explore the lush tea estates and relish the panoramic views of the Western Ghats. Return to your hotel and relax. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady

Following breakfast, proceed to Thekkady, known for its wildlife sanctuary and spice plantations. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and freshen up. Later, visit the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary for an exciting boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you can spot diverse wildlife amidst the dense forests. Return to your hotel and unwind. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 4: Thekkady to Alleppey Houseboat

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey, renowned for its enchanting backwaters. Board your traditional houseboat and embark on a leisurely cruise through the serene backwaters of Alleppey. Marvel at the scenic beauty around you and savor delicious Kerala cuisine served onboard. Witness a captivating sunset over the backwaters. Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 5: Alleppey to Kochi

Disembark from the houseboat after breakfast and proceed to Kochi. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. In the afternoon, explore the cultural landmarks of Kochi, including the Chinese Fishing Nets, Mattancherry Palace, and Jewish Synagogue. Enjoy an enthralling Kathakali dance performance in the evening. Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 6: Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, to Kochi Airport/Railway Station for your return journey to Delhi/Chandigarh. Depart with cherished memories of your serene getaway to Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey, and Kochi.
