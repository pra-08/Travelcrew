Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh->Jaipur

Commence your journey to the vibrant city of Jaipur by boarding the Volvo from the designated pick-up point in Delhi/Chandigarh in the night. Experience the comfort of the Volvo ride as you head towards the Pink City.

Day 2:Jaipur Sightseeing

Arrive in Jaipur in the morning and check-in at the hotel. After breakfast and some relaxation, embark on a tour of the city's iconic landmarks. Explore the majestic Amber Fort, the stunning Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), the historic City Palace, and the astronomical wonders at Jantar Mantar. Later, delve into the vibrant colors and bustling markets of the Old City. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3:Jaipur to Udaipur

In the morning, depart for the enchanting city of Udaipur. En route, visit the renowned Ranakpur Jain Temple, famous for its intricate marble architecture. Upon arrival in Udaipur, check-in at the hotel and spend the evening at leisure, exploring the lakeside city or indulging in a relaxing boat ride on Lake Pichola. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4:Udaipur Sightseeing

Begin your day with a visit to the majestic City Palace, followed by the serene Saheliyon ki Bari (Garden of Maidens). Explore the historic Jagdish Temple and enjoy panoramic views of the city from the Monsoon Palace. Later, immerse yourself in the local culture with a visit to the vibrant markets and artisanal shops. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5:Udaipur to Jaisalmer

Today, embark on a scenic journey to the golden city of Jaisalmer. En route, stop at the fascinating Kumbhalgarh Fort, known for its impressive architecture and panoramic views of the Aravalli Range. Upon arrival in Jaisalmer, check-in at the hotel and unwind. Spend the evening exploring the bustling streets of the ancient fort or enjoying a camel safari amidst the golden sand dunes. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.p>

Day 6:Jailsalmer Sighteeing

Explore the mesmerizing Jaisalmer Fort, known for its intricate carvings and stunning architecture. Visit the Jain temples within the fort complex and discover the ancient havelis (mansions) adorned with exquisite artwork. Later, witness the magical sunset at the famous Sam Sand Dunes and partake in cultural performances under the starlit sky. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7:Departure

After breakfast, bid farewell to the golden city of Jaisalmer as you head back home via Volvo. Reflect on the unforgettable memories and experiences gathered during your journey through Rajasthan's cultural gems.
