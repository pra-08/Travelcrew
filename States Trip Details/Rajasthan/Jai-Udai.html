Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh to Jaipur

Commence your journey from the bustling city of Delhi/Chandigarh towards the Pink City of Jaipur. Board the Volvo from the designated pick-up point and enjoy a comfortable overnight journey.

Day 2:Jaipur Arrival & Sightseeing

Arrive in Jaipur in the morning and check-in at the hotel. After refreshing yourself, embark on a full day of sightseeing in Jaipur. Explore the majestic Amber Fort, marvel at the intricate designs of Hawa Mahal, visit the historic City Palace, and witness the astronomical wonders at Jantar Mantar. Spend your evening strolling through the vibrant markets of the Old City. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Jaipur.

Day 3:Jaipur to Udaipur via Chittorgarh

After breakfast, begin your journey towards the romantic city of Udaipur. En route, stop at the majestic Chittorgarh Fort, a symbol of Rajput valor and sacrifice. Continue your drive to Udaipur and upon arrival, check-in at the hotel. Spend the evening relaxing by the picturesque Lake Pichola. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Udaipur.

Day 4: Udaipur Sightseeing

Explore the enchanting city of Udaipur, starting with a visit to the iconic City Palace, showcasing exquisite Rajasthani and Mughal architecture. Enjoy a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola, admiring the stunning Lake Palace and Jag Mandir. Visit the Saheliyon ki Bari and the intricate Jagdish Temple. In the evening, witness a cultural performance at Bagore ki Haveli. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, bid farewell to Udaipur as you head back to Delhi/Chandigarh via Volvo. Depart with fond memories of the rich heritage and vibrant culture of Rajasthan.
