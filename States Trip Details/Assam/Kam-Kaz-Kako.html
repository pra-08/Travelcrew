Day 1:Departure from Patna to Guwahati

Embark on an exciting journey from Patna to Guwahati, the gateway to the enchanting state of Assam. Board the designated transport and begin your scenic drive through the picturesque landscapes of Bihar and Assam. Enjoy the journey as you traverse towards your first destination. Upon arrival in Guwahati, check-in at the hotel and unwind. Spend the evening exploring the local markets or indulging in delicious Assamese cuisine. Dinner and overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 2:Guwahati to Kamakhya Temple and Kakochang Waterfall

After breakfast, proceed to visit the revered Kamakhya Temple, one of the most important Shakti Peethas in India, located atop Nilachal Hill. Seek blessings at the temple and soak in the spiritual ambiance. Later, head towards the picturesque Kakochang Waterfall, nestled amidst lush greenery and serene surroundings. Enjoy the natural beauty and spend some time relaxing by the waterfall. Return to Guwahati in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3:Guwahati to Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary

After breakfast, depart for Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary, renowned for its dense population of one-horned rhinoceros. Embark on an exhilarating jeep safari through the sanctuary, offering opportunities to spot various wildlife species including rhinoceros, elephants, wild buffaloes, and migratory birds. Enjoy the thrill of wildlife viewing amidst the natural habitats of Assam. Return to Guwahati in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4:Guwahati to Kaziranga National Park

After breakfast, proceed to Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site and home to the highest population of one-horned rhinoceros in the world. Upon arrival, check-in at the resort and freshen up. Embark on an adventurous jeep safari through the wilderness of Kaziranga, exploring the diverse flora and fauna of the park. Keep your camera ready to capture sightings of rhinoceros, tigers, elephants, and various bird species. Return to the resort for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5:Kaziranga National Park to Kaziranga National Orchid

After breakfast, visit the Kaziranga National Orchid and Biodiversity Park, showcasing the rich biodiversity of the region through its extensive collection of orchids and other rare plant species. Explore the park's lush gardens, greenhouses, and walking trails, learning about the unique flora of Assam. Spend the day surrounded by the vibrant colors and fragrances of the orchids. Return to the resort in Kaziranga for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 6:Departure from Kaziranga to Patna

After breakfast, bid farewell to the captivating landscapes of Assam as you begin your return journey to Patna. Depart with fond memories of your Assam trip, filled with spiritual experiences, wildlife encounters, and natural wonders.
