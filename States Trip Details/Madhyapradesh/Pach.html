Day 1:Delhi/Chandigarh to Pachmarhi

Embark on a scenic journey from Delhi/Chandigarh to the charming hill station of Pachmarhi. Board the Volvo from the designated pick-up point and enjoy a comfortable overnight journey.

Day 2:Arrival in Pachmarhi

Arrive in Pachmarhi in the morning and check-in at the hotel. After freshening up, start your exploration of this picturesque hill station. Visit the popular sightseeing spots including Rajat Prapat (Big Waterfall), Bee Fall, and Duchess Falls, where you can marvel at the breathtaking natural beauty and enjoy the serene ambiance. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Pachmarhi.

Day 3:Pachmarhi-Jatashankar Trek

After breakfast, gear up for an adventurous trek to Jatashankar. Begin the trek through the lush forests and scenic trails, surrounded by the beauty of nature. En route, visit the beautiful Apsara Vihar waterfall and take a refreshing dip in its crystal-clear waters. Continue the trek to reach Jatashankar, a sacred cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, nestled amidst the natural rock formations. Explore the surroundings and soak in the spiritual ambiance. Trek back to Pachmarhi in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4:Pachmarhi - Dhoopgarh Trek

Start your day with another exciting trek to Dhoopgarh, the highest point in the Satpura Range. Begin the trek early to witness a mesmerizing sunrise from the summit. Trek through the dense forests and rugged terrain, enjoying the panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes. Reach Dhoopgarh and marvel at the breathtaking vistas of the lush valleys and distant hills. Spend some time exploring the area and capturing the scenic beauty. Trek back to Pachmarhi in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5:Departure from Pachmarhi

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene landscapes of Pachmarhi as you head back to Delhi/Chandigarh via Volvo. Depart with cherished memories of your adventurous trekking experience and the enchanting hill station of Pachmarhi.
