Day 1:Arrival in Coimbatore - Transfer to Ooty

Your exclusive trip to Tamil Nadu begins with your arrival in Coimbatore. Our representative will greet you at the airport or railway station and drive you to Ooty, also known as the Queen of Hill Stations. Check into your hotel and relax after the journey. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the charming hill town. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 2:Ooty Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Ooty. Visit the Doddabetta Peak, the highest point in the Nilgiris, for breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills. Explore the Government Botanical Garden, home to a diverse range of exotic plants and flowers. Enjoy a scenic boat ride on Ooty Lake and visit the picturesque Rose Garden. Return to your hotel and unwind. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 3:Ooty to Coonoor

After breakfast, drive to Coonoor, another beautiful hill station near Ooty. Visit Sim's Park, a botanical garden known for its unique collection of plants and trees. Enjoy a ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, from Coonoor to Ooty, offering stunning views of the Nilgiri Hills. Explore the tea gardens and sample freshly brewed tea at a local estate. Return to Ooty in the evening. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 4:Ooty to Madurai

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and drive to Madurai, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a cultural hub of Tamil Nadu. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. In the afternoon, visit the iconic Meenakshi Amman Temple, dedicated to the goddess Meenakshi and known for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings. Explore the bustling streets of the surrounding bazaars. Overnight stay in Madurai.

Day 5:Madurai to Rameswaram

After breakfast, proceed to Rameswaram, one of the holiest pilgrimage sites for Hindus and home to the famous Ramanathaswamy Temple. Check into your hotel and freshen up. Visit the Ramanathaswamy Temple, known for its magnificent corridors and sacred water tanks. Take a holy dip in the waters of Agni Theertham. Explore other attractions such as Dhanushkodi and Pamban Bridge. Overnight stay in Rameswaram.

Day 6:Rameswaram to Kanyakumari

After breakfast, drive to Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of mainland India, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet. Check into your hotel and unwind. Visit the Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue, situated on a rocky island off the coast. Enjoy breathtaking views of the sunrise and sunset from the confluence point. Overnight stay in Kanyakumari.

Day 7:Kanyakumari to Kodaikanal

After breakfast, drive to Kodaikanal, a serene hill station nestled in the Western Ghats. Check into your hotel and relax. Visit the Coakers Walk, a picturesque promenade offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys. Explore the enchanting Bryant Park and the serene Kodai Lake. Enjoy boating and horse riding activities. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.

Day 8:Kodaikanal to Coimbatore - Departure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and drive to Coimbatore. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. Depart with fond memories of your exclusive trip to Tamil Nadu, having explored its diverse landscapes, rich culture, and spiritual heritage.
