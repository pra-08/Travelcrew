Day 1:Departure from Delhi/Chandigarh to Guwahati

Your journey to Meghalaya begins with a flight from Delhi/Chandigarh to Guwahati. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Check-in, relax, and prepare for your upcoming adventure in Meghalaya. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 2:Guwahati to Shillong

After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive from Guwahati to Shillong. En route, visit the picturesque Umiam Lake for a serene boating experience. Upon arrival in Shillong, check into your hotel and unwind. In the evening, explore Police Bazaar and enjoy local delicacies. Overnight stay in Shillong.

Day 3:Trek to David Scott Trail

Today, start your day with an early breakfast and proceed for a trek to the David Scott Trail, a historic route used during the British era. Trek through lush forests, rolling hills, and quaint villages. Marvel at the scenic beauty and panoramic views along the trail. Return to Shillong in the evening and relax. Overnight stay in Shillong.

Day 4:Shillong to Cherrapunji

After breakfast, drive to Cherrapunji, known as the wettest place on earth. En route, visit the Mawphlang Sacred Grove, a dense forest with unique biodiversity. Upon arrival in Cherrapunji, check into your hotel and rest. In the evening, visit the Seven Sisters Falls and enjoy the breathtaking views. Overnight stay in Cherrapunji.

Day 5:Trek to Nongriat Village and Living Root Bridges

Today's adventure takes you on a trek to Nongriat Village, known for its stunning living root bridges. Begin the trek from Tyrna village and descend around 3,500 steps to reach Nongriat. Explore the picturesque village and marvel at the living root bridges, a unique natural phenomenon. Return to Cherrapunji in the evening. Overnight stay in Cherrapunji.

Day 6:Cherrapunji to Mawlynnong

After breakfast, drive to Mawlynnong, acclaimed as the cleanest village in Asia. Explore the village, interact with the locals, and admire the cleanliness initiatives. Visit the iconic Living Root Bridge and enjoy a leisurely walk through the village. In the evening, witness a mesmerizing sunset from the Sky Walk viewpoint. Overnight stay in Mawlynnong.

Day 7:Trek to Double Decker Living Root Bridge

Embark on a trek to the Double Decker Living Root Bridge near Nongriat Village. This majestic bridge is a marvel of nature and requires a moderate trek to reach. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding forests and streams as you trek through the lush greenery. Return to Mawlynnong in the evening. Overnight stay in Mawlynnong.

Day 8:Mawlynnong to Guwahati

After breakfast, bid farewell to the serene beauty of Meghalaya as you drive back to Guwahati. Depending on your flight schedule, you may have some free time to explore Guwahati's attractions. Later, transfer to the Guwahati Airport for your onward journey back to Delhi/Chandigarh, carrying with you cherished memories of your trekking adventure in Meghalaya.
