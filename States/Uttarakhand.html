Welcome to Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a state in northern India, often referred to as the "Land of the Gods" due to its numerous Hindu temples and pilgrimage sites. It was carved out of the Himalayan and adjoining northwestern districts of Uttar Pradesh in 2000 and became the 27th state of the Indian Union. The state is known for its stunning natural beauty, with majestic mountains, lush valleys, rivers, and forests.
