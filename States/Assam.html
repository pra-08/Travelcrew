Welcome to Assam

Assam is a state in northeastern India, known for its rich cultural heritage, lush landscapes, and biodiversity. It is one of the seven sister states in the region. Assam is famous for its tea plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, and the Brahmaputra River, which flows through the heart of the state. In terms of domain names, entities based in Assam can use the .in domain to denote their Indian affiliation. Similarly, they can use .com or other generic top-level domains (TLDs) for their websites, depending on their preferences and the nature of their online presence.
