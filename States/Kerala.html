Welcome to Kerala

Kerala is a state located on the southwestern coast of India, known as the "God's Own Country" due to its scenic beauty and rich cultural heritage. It is renowned for its backwaters, palm-lined beaches, lush greenery, and diverse wildlife. Kerala is also famous for its Ayurvedic treatments, traditional art forms such as Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, and delicious cuisine. In terms of domain names, entities based in Kerala can use the .in domain to denote their Indian affiliation. Similarly, they can use .com or other generic top-level domains (TLDs) for their websites, depending on their preferences and the nature of their online presence.
