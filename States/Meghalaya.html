Welcome to Meghalaya

Meghalaya is a state in northeastern India, known for its picturesque landscapes, lush forests, and abundant rainfall. It is bordered by Assam to the north and east and Bangladesh to the south and west. Meghalaya is famous for its scenic hill stations, cascading waterfalls, living root bridges, and unique tribal cultures. In terms of domain names, entities based in Meghalaya can use the .in domain to denote their Indian affiliation. Similarly, they can use .com or other generic top-level domains (TLDs) for their websites, depending on their preferences and the nature of their online presence.
