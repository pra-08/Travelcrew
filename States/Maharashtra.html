Welcome to Maharashtra

Maharashtra is a diverse state located in the western part of India, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and economic significance. It is the third-largest state in terms of area and the second-most populous state in India. The capital of Maharashtra is Mumbai, which is not only the financial and commercial capital of India but also a melting pot of cultures and lifestyles. Mumbai is famous for its bustling streets, iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive, and its vibrant film industry, Bollywood.
